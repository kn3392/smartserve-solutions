Online Service Management System

SmartServe Solutions provides a centralized web-based platform where customers can request services, track service status in real time, and make secure online payments.

System Features

The system is designed to automate service management and improve customer satisfaction.

Online Service Request

Customers can submit service requests online without manual paperwork.

Service Tracking

Customers can track service status and receive real-time updates.

Secure Online Payment

The system supports secure digital payment methods for service fees.

Efficient Service Management

Admins can assign technicians and monitor service operations efficiently.

System Users

Customer

Requests services, tracks status, and makes online payments.

Service Manager (Admin)

Manages service requests, assigns technicians, and monitors progress.

Technician

Views assigned tasks and updates service completion status.
